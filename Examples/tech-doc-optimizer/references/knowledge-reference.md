## 2. Industry Standards

### Technical Documentation Quality Assessment Dimensions
- **Factual Consistency**: Cross-document consistency of entities, relationships, and values
- **Logical Coherence**: Completeness of causal chains, degree of premise–conclusion matching
- **Format Governance**: Degree of standardization of numbering, headings, metadata, and terminology
- **Executability**: Clarity of operational instructions, completeness of steps

### Integrity Verification Dimensions
- Missing documentation checks
- Content coverage
- Mapping relationship completeness
- Index consistency
- Logical coherence (avoiding gaps and contradictions)
